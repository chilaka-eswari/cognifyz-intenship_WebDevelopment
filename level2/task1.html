Web Development

Web development plays a crucial role in shaping the digital landscape of today's world. It involves the creation and maintenance of websites and web applications, utilizing a variety of technologies and frameworks. Technologies like Node.js, Python and Ruby on Rails are commonly used for back-end development, handling tasks such as data processing, authentication, and server communication.

Web development encompasses the multifaceted process of building, designing, and maintaining websites and web applications. It represents the amalgamation of various disciplines, including front-end, back-end, and full-stack development. Front-end development involves crafting the user interface and experience using languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
